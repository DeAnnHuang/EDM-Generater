[Video] IoT Devices as Jumping-off Points for Cybercrime: Trend Micro Security Predictions for 2018

In 2018, cybercriminals will explore new ways to abuse IoT devices for their own gain. We expect more IoT vulnerabilities being discovered in the market as many, if not most, devices are manufactured without hardened security. The increasing connectivity and expanded attack surface afforded by the internet of things present opportunities for cybercriminals looking to hijack devices and hack into networks.
